    The Department of Management Sciences

    The Department presently offers an M.B.A. Degree Programme approved by the AICTE, Government of Andhra Pradesh, with affiliation from Acharya Nagarjuna University. The Faculty uses effective pedagogic tools that include classroom lectures supported by audio visual aids, group discussions and seminars, case studies to make the learning effective and comprehensive. Besides, the Department places special emphasis on Guest Lectures by eminent business executives and senior faculty drawn from the professional institutes of Management at the national level.

    The MANMEET - 2024

    22nd Ramarayudu Memorial Management Meet

    (National-Level B-Schools Meet)
    On February 15, 2024

    The Department organizes every year MANMEET - Management Students Meet - with the main objectives:

    • To provide a unique opportunity to the third millennium young budding Managers to test their competencies.
    • To encourage creative spark, entrepreneurial spirit, and innovative competencies in the young future managers for facing up managerial challenges in the ever-changing and dynamic era.
    • To provide a level playing field for the prospective Managers to interact with their peers from sister colleges and Universities in the State.

  • Events

    Events

    EVENTS

    Business Quiz

    It enables to test the business quotient level of the participants, knowledge of various functions of business and test the pace of their acquaintance with business environment (Preliminary & Final).


    Note : Number of participants - Three.

    Market Makers

    Teams have to develop a product concept, scan the environment, develop a successful marketing strategy (not restricted only to marketing mix) for the concept and sell the concept to the target audience with innovative, intellectual skills as they try to attract the attention of target audience in real life business situation. (Preliminary & Final).


    Note : Number of participants - Three.

    Smart Manager

    Stress and strain are two common things for a business strategist. The event aims at testing one's own analytical abilities, functional competencies, convincing capabilities, selling skills, attitudes etc. (Preliminary & Final).


    Note : Number of participants - One.

    Dalaal Ka Kamaal

    Simulated stock market game which aims to test the knowledge of stock market and ability to predict and pick up stocks with limited resources, constructing a portfolio and getting maximum returns from fluctuating prices due to uncertain conditions in the market. (Preliminary & Final).


    Note : Number of participants - Three.


    Note :

    1. Any number of teams from a college can participate.

    2. No registration fee
