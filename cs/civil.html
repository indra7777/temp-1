  • Model Making

    Model Making

    MODEL MAKING

    Description:

    Bridge model making competition is to showcase the skills in designing aesthetically and economically in the design of the bridge. The model will be fabricated by the supplied materials only and tested for the strength-to-weight ratio.

    Rules & Guidelines

    1. It is a team event with a minimum of 2 members with an objective of fabricating a bridge model of their choice in a fixed time.
    2. Sticks (unlimited), Gum, Cutter, and Thread only will be allowed for the fabrication and are provided at the time of the event.
    3. A selection criterion is based on Architecture, Dead weight, and Maximum load measured at 30 cm span, 11 cm width, and length not less than 40 cm.
    4. A maximum of 10 teams will be shortlisted if more teams are registered for the event by a screening test comprises of short answers/multiple choice questions in Solid mechanics, Structural analysis and other general topics in civil engineering.
    5. Duration of model making- 1 hr 30 Min + 10 min Grace
    6. Failure load to the dead weight of the structure is used to assess the efficiency of the model.
